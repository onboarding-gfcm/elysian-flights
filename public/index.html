Frequently Asked Questions

How does Élysian Flights work? ▼
We search award availability across multiple airline loyalty programs to find Business and First Class flights at a fraction of the retail price. Once you find a flight, contact us and we'll handle the booking.
What are award flights? ▼
Award flights are booked using airline miles or points instead of cash. This can save you up to 80% compared to retail business class fares.
Which airlines do you cover? ▼
We search Emirates, Etihad, KLM, and Air France through Qantas, Flying Blue, Virgin Atlantic, and American Airlines loyalty programs.
How do I book a flight? ▼
Simply search for your route, find a flight you like, and contact us via WhatsApp. We'll guide you through the entire booking process.
