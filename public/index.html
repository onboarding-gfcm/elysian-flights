Questions

Frequently Asked

How does Élysian Flights work? +
We search award availability across multiple airline loyalty programs to find Business and First Class flights at a fraction of the retail price. Once you find a flight, contact us and we'll handle the booking.
What are award flights? +
Award flights are booked using airline miles or points instead of cash. This can save you up to 80% compared to retail business class fares. We find the best redemption options across multiple programs.
Which airlines do you cover? +
We search Emirates, Etihad, KLM, and Air France through Qantas, Flying Blue, Virgin Atlantic, and American Airlines loyalty programs. This gives us access to a wide range of premium cabin availability worldwide.
How do I book a flight? +
Simply search for your route, find a flight you like, and contact us via WhatsApp. We'll guide you through the entire booking process and ensure you get the best deal.
Do I need my own miles or points? +
No, you don't need existing miles. We handle the points acquisition and booking on your behalf. You simply pay our fee and we take care of everything else to get you in that premium cabin.
